# Butternut Squash Soup
### Ingredients
|Quantity|Ingredient|
----------:|:-------
|1|butternut squash|
|1|large onion|
|4|cans non fat low salt chicken broth|
|as needed| soy sauce, cumin, cayenne pepper, paprika|

### Instructions

* Bake squash at 250 degree F oven for 40 minutes. Cut in half, remove seeds, and peel. Cut into pieces.
* Saut√© sliced onion, add soy sauce, cumin, cayenne pepper, and paprika, to taste. Add chicken broth. Cook for approx. 30 minutes. Then puree in food processor or blender.
